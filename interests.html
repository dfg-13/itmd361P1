Sports that I like

I enjoy keeping up and watching Formula 1. I didn't actually develop an interest in this up until the end of the 2019 season. What sparked an interest was actually messing with racing simulators and using Formula-1 spec cars. Gaining a perspective of what the drivers do via simulator allowed me to gain an initial understanding of what drivers have to do and endure during a race.

I am a big Fernando Alonso fan. I find that his cheeky and troll-like mannerisms to be very amusing. From blackmailing the Ron Dennis (Mclaren CEO) in 2007 to having angry radio communications during his second Mclaren stint. I would regard him as the GOAT mainly because he has been performing at a high level for 20 consistent years from 2001 to present day.
