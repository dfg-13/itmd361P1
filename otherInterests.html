Other Interests I Have

For the sake of keeping it short in case you don't want to read or scroll. I am into video games often playing Dota 2 or messing around in racing simulators. I am also into a somewhat "niche" genre of electronic music.

Video Games

Dota 2

As far as video games go, I really only stick to Dota 2 and like two or three racing simulator titles. Five years ago, I probably had more games to play, but as time went on I just grew bored of other titles or I just realized I would rather put time on the titles I truly enjoy.

First up, I am very into Dota 2. It is a MOBA competitive esport title. The main objective of the game is to destroy the other teams "ancient" which is a special type of building. Games can vary in length with some going as short as 25-minutes with others going as long as 1.5 hours. Although the long games are rare, they do happen.

I have about just a little less than 4,000 hours on the game. I started playing the game back in 2015, and it wasn't until the 1000th hour that I started gettng better. I mainly play as a "position 4" (soft support) which is the support to the offlaner hero. I also rotate between a few heroes which are Ancient Apparition, Lich, and Phoenix.

Racing Simulators

Aside from playing a competitive title like Dota 2 which can be mentally exhausting at times. I also enjoy playing around in racing simulators such as Assetto Corsa, Assetto Corsa Competitizione, and Automobilista 2. I also like other titles, but these three I just spend the most time on.

What appeals the most to me about racing simulators is feeling the how different cars from different disciplines of racing react to different tracks as well as modifying certain parts of a setup like a car's suspension or aerodynamic properties. Furthermore, I love reading into data and statistics, and the fact that these simulators extract data from telemetry also make it more realistic since real physics are implemented into these simulators.
